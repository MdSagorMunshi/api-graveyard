# Code of Conduct

This project records losses developers actually lived through. That only works if the record is trustworthy.

Rule one: every entry must be verifiably true. If you claim a service died, was paywalled, was acquired into irrelevance, or had its limits gutted, bring a source. Official announcements are best. Archived copies are acceptable. Rumor is not.

Rule two: eulogies must be original writing. Do not paste marketing copy, press coverage, or AI output. The project has a voice on purpose, and that voice depends on people meaning what they write.

Rule three: treat other contributors with respect. Correct facts directly. Disagree with evidence. Do not turn review into theater.

Maintainers may close or remove contributions that are inaccurate, plagiarized, abusive, or clearly not made in good faith.

This is a place of memory. We take that seriously.
